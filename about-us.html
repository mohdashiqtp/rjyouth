The programme was formally inaugurated by honourable Dr. Manmohan Singh, Formar Prime Minister of India By lightening lamp and this was followed by the inaugural address to the esteemed audience. The keynote address was delivered by Sri K.V Venugopal, General Secretory, AICC. He also released a stamp in the name of Rajiv Gandhi and the same was recived by Sri Joseph Vazhakkan formar MLA.
